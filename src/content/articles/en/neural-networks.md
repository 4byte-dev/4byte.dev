Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) are computational models inspired by the biological neural networks in the human brain. They are considered one of the most powerful tools in machine learning.

## Core Components

A neural network consists of three main layers:

- **Input Layer**: The layer where data enters the network
- **Hidden Layers**: Layers where features are learned
- **Output Layer**: The layer where results are produced

## How They Work

Neural networks process data through layers to identify patterns. Each neuron multiplies inputs by weights and passes through an activation function.

- Data propagates forward (forward propagation)
- Error is calculated (loss calculation)
- Weights are updated via backpropagation (backpropagation)

## Applications

Successfully used in image recognition, natural language processing, speech recognition, and game playing.
